### SSC Perma Form Storage

An addon for Shape Shifter Curse (mod for Minecraft 1.20.1) that allows reverting permanent transformation and storing their forms for later use.
It's still a very early WIP while I learn how to mod Minecraft at all.

### The plan:
- The mod will add two new items:
- - **Gilded Echo Shard** - crafted in an anvil by adding a Gold Block to an Echo Shard.
- - **Cracked Gilded Echo Shard** - can be repaired and turned back into a usable form in an anvil by adding a Gold Ingot to it.
- The _Gilded Echo Shard_ will allow the player to change back into a human while you're in a permanent form. By doing so, the Shard will contain the permanent form (through an item tag). In that state, it will allow the player to use it to regain that form. After that use, it will turn into a _Cracked Gilded Echo Shard_.
- The player can use the _Gilded Echo Shard_ to store a permanent form by holding it in off-hand and eating a _Powerful Inhibitor_. To regain the stored permanent form, the player must be in human form and eat the Shard itself.
- The transformations caused by the _Gilded Echo Shards_ happen by executing Shape Shifter Curse's commands.
- Overall, the player will be able to collect and change between permanent forms at the cost of gold and w.
